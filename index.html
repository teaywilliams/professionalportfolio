ABOUT ME

Hello my name is Teayarnna Williams, I am currently a Web Development Student at Eleven Fifty Academy. If someone was to ask me my hobbies, I take an interest in always finding a way to help those who are within arms reach.I have spent six years weaving through educational positions geared towards assisting those who are our most vulnerable learners.
After an excessive time in the Educational Field, I realize that as technology advanced it still a large gap of opportunities, resources and knowledge that isn’t being presented to vulnerable communities.I am attending Eleven Fifty in hopes to one day work with teams of developers who’s passion is to design spaces on the web for folks who need to be seen in rooms they could never picture themselves in.
You can find my code on Github, and learn more about my experience on LinkedIn. Also I have a podcast I started and I am still trying to committed the much needed time to it. My podcast is “Letters Back Home” it a personal digital journal to remind myself that I am connected and connecting my creativity with the intersection of all my identities and those around the world.
